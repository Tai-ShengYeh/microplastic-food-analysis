Warm-up: How many microplastics does an average adult ingest per week?

Research findings

Senathirajah et al. (2021) systematic review estimated that adults ingest 0.1–5 g per week (median 0.7 g/week) of microplastics — equivalent to the plastic mass of a credit card. Bottled water exposure is 3–4× higher than tap water.

Senathirajah K. et al. (2021) J Hazard Mater 404:124004.

What are microplastics?

微塑膠 (Microplastics, MP)：粒徑 < 5 mm 的塑膠顆粒（由 NOAA / GESAMP 定義；歐盟提案 1 μm – 5 mm).

奈米塑膠 (Nanoplastics, NP)：粒徑 < 1 μm（或 < 100 nm 嚴格定義)，來自微塑膠的進一步破碎或產品中的奈米添加.

Primary MP

Manufactured directly as small particles: facial scrubs, toothpaste, industrial resin pellets, tire-wear particles, cosmetics.

Secondary MP

Larger plastics broken down in the environment by UV, heat, or mechanical forces: bottle fragments, fishing-net debris, degraded agricultural film, fibers shed during laundry.

Cole M. et al. (2011) Microplastics as contaminants in the marine environment: a review. Mar Pollut Bull 62:2588–2597.

Exposure routes for microplastics in food

Water and beverages

  • Bottled water: 325 particles/L (median, Mason 2018)
  • Tap water: 5.5 particles/L
  • Beer and ready-to-drink tea are also positive

Sea salt and seasonings

  • Sea salt: 550–681 particles/kg (Yang 2015)
  • Lake salt and rock salt: lower content
  • Mainly PET, PE, PP

Seafood and shellfish

  • Mussels: 0.36 particles/g soft tissue
  • Oysters and clams accumulate suspended MP
  • Most reside in the digestive tract

Packaging migration and brewing

  • Plastic tea bags (PA/PET): ~1.16 × 10⁹ NP per cup (Hernandez 2019)
  • Infant feeding bottles (PP) at 80°C: 1.6 × 10⁶ particles/L (Li 2020)

Hernandez LM. et al. (2019) Plastic Teabags Release Billions of Microparticles and Nanoparticles into Tea. Environ Sci Technol 53:12300–12310.

Why should food analysts care?

Particle-based effects

  • Nanoplastics can cross the intestinal wall and have been detected in blood, placenta, liver, and kidneys
  • Cellular: oxidative stress, inflammation, mitochondrial dysfunction
  • Leslie et al. (2022) first reported MP in human blood

Chemical co-contaminants

  • Plasticizers: phthalate esters (DEHP, DBP) — analyzed by LC-MS
  • Bisphenol A (BPA): endocrine disruptor
  • Antioxidants, UV stabilizers: phenolic additives
  • Environmentally adsorbed PCBs and pesticide residues can be carried by MP

Leslie HA. et al. (2022) Discovery and quantification of plastic particle pollution in human blood. Environ Int 163:107199.

Vibrational SpectroscopyPrinciple：分子在「跳什麼舞」?

Energy levels

When molecules absorb IR light, vibrational energy levels transition from ground to excited states. Each chemical bond (C–H, C=O, C–O, C–Cl) has a characteristic frequency.

ν̃ (cm⁻¹) = 1/λ

Mid-infrared (MIR): ~4000–400 cm⁻¹; near-infrared (NIR): ~12500–4000 cm⁻¹.

Selection rules: absorption vs scattering

  • IR-active: vibration causes change in dipole moment → strong for C=O, O–H
  • Raman-active: vibration causes change in polarizability → strong for C=C, aromatic rings
  • For centrosymmetric molecules the two are complementary (mutual exclusion)

Near-infrared (NIR) spectroscopy: rapid screening

Principle

NIR spans 12500–4000 cm⁻¹（800–2500 nm)，primaryRecords the C–H、O–H、N–H 鍵的overtones and combination bands.

Broad peaks, weak signal, low resolution → requires chemometrics（PCA、PLS、SIMCA)建模辨識.

Role in food microplastic analysis

  • 大尺寸塑膠 (> 1 mm) recycling-line sorting is the mainstream technology
  • Large-particle screening: HSI (hyperspectral imaging) 1000–2500 nm
  • Not suitable < 500 μm MP 分析
  • 食品基質干擾大（水、油的 O–H、C–H 強吸收)

PE 在 NIR 的特徵：~1210、1410、1730 nm（C–H 第一倍頻)，~2310 nm（C–H 組合頻).本圖為實驗室自量數據，未做基線校正，因此 2300 nm 以後吸收急遽上升（Sample 厚度與儀器響應).

Serranti S. et al. (2018) The utilization of hyperspectral imaging for impurities detection in secondary plastics. Open Waste Manag J.

Fourier-transform infrared spectroscopy (FTIR): the workhorse for microplastics

關鍵Principle

  • 光源（globar)→ Michelson interferometer → sample → detector
  • Records the interferogram
  • Fourier transform → absorption spectrum
  • All wavenumbers measured simultaneously → Fellgett multiplex SNR advantage
  • Explanation度可達 0.5 cm⁻¹

顯微 FTIR (μ-FTIR) and 化學成像

傳統單點顯微 FTIR：detection limits約 10–20 μm（受 IR 繞射極限限制).

Using a FPA 偵測器（Focal Plane Array) enables 64 × 64 pixel chemical imaging in one shot; whole filters can be auto-scanned for chemical classification（搭配 siMPle、PRIMPKE 等 open-source 軟體).

QCL-IR: the next-generation technology

  • Quantum cascade laser (QCL) source
  • High intensity, enabling fast scanning — a 1 cm² filter can be analyzed in 30 minutes
  • detection limits可達 ~5 μm

O-PTIR (Optical photothermal IR)

  • Combines IR source with visible probe
  • Breaks the diffraction limit, reaching ~ 500 nm 空間Explanation度
  • Simultaneous Raman possible; high instrument cost

FTIR 圖譜辨識（一)：請判斷塑膠類型

Reasoning
  • PE is correct：2915（CH₂ 不對稱伸縮)+ 2848（CH₂ 對稱伸縮)+ 1463（CH₂ 剪式變角)+ 720（CH₂ 搖動雙峰，分裂為 730/720 是 HDPE 結晶特徵)— 這是 PE 的「Four characteristic peaks」.
  • Not PP：PP 一定要看到 1377 cm⁻¹（CH₃ symmetric bend)與 998/974/840 cm⁻¹ 三個骨架峰.
  • Not PET：缺少 1715 cm⁻¹ 強烈的 C=O 吸收.
  • Not PS：缺少 3025 cm⁻¹ 芳香 C–H、1601/1493 苯環、696 cm⁻¹.

FTIR 圖譜辨識（二)：海鹽中分離的 80 μm 透明顆粒

Reasoning
  • PET is correct: 1715 cm⁻¹ is the strong ester C=O absorption的標記；1240（C–O–C 不對稱伸縮)+ 1090（C–O 對稱伸縮)+ 720（對位取代苯 C–H 變角)構成 PET 的標準四峰群.
  • Nylon (PA) also has C=O, but its amide C=O lies near 1640 cm⁻¹（amide I)，且伴隨 3300 cm⁻¹ 寬 N–H 峰，與 PET 不同.
  • PVC 的標誌是 690 cm⁻¹ 強 C–Cl stretch，本譜沒看到.
  • A particle of this type isolated from sea salt likely originates from oceanic PET bottle fragments，符合 Yang et al. (2015) 在中國市售食鹽中發現以 PET 為主的觀察.

Yang D. et al. (2015) Microplastic Pollution in Table Salts from China. Environ Sci Technol 49:13622–13627.

Raman spectroscopy: the power of scattering

關鍵Principle

  • Monochromatic laser illuminates the sample (commonly 532, 633, 785, or 1064 nm)
  • Most light undergoes Rayleigh scattering（彈性，波長不變)
  • A small fraction undergoes Raman scattering（非彈性，波長變化等於分子振動能量)
  • Stokes line ν₀ − νᵥ; anti-Stokes line ν₀ + νᵥ
Δν̃ = (1/λ₀) − (1/λₛ)

Two common Raman challenges

Issue 1: fluorescence interference

Food matrices, colored particles, and additive-loaded plastics emit strong fluorescence background，淹沒拉曼訊號.

Remedies

  • Switch to 785 nm or 1064 nm 雷射（光子能量低，較少激發螢光)
  • Sample pre-bleaching（H₂O₂ + UV)
  • SERDS (Shifted-Excitation Raman Difference Spectroscopy)
  • 數學基線校正（asymmetric least squares)

Issue 2: nanoplastics too small

常規 Raman 無法看到 < 1 μm 顆粒.

Remedies

  • SERS (Surface-Enhanced Raman)：將Sample 吸附於 Au/Ag 奈米基板上，訊號增強 10⁶–10¹⁰ 倍，可看到單分子
  • TERS (Tip-Enhanced)：結合 AFM 探針，Explanation度 ~20 nm
  • O-PTIR: reaches 500 nm, with simultaneous Raman acquisition

Xu G. et al. (2020) Surface-Enhanced Raman Spectroscopy facilitates the detection of microplastics <1 μm in the environment. Environ Sci Technol 54:15594–15603.

Why mass spectrometry? Is spectroscopy not enough?

Inherent limitations of spectroscopy

  • Quantification unit is "particles per filter area"，不是「mass / Sample 重」
  • 奈米塑膠 (< 1 μm) 顆粒太多，個別計數不切實際
  • Sample 基質干擾大時，無法成像
  • Cannot distinguish additives and oligomers

How MS fills the gap

  • Py-GC-MS / TED-GC-MS: pyrolyze plastic to small molecules; quantify by GC-MS to obtain mass-based quantification (μg)
  • LC-MS：分析Plasticizers、抗氧化劑、BPA、寡聚物
  • NP 範圍仍可分析（Sample 濃縮即可)
  • Food matrix can be injected directly after digestion

Complementarity

Spectroscopy answers "What is it? How many?"; mass spectrometry answers "它有多少mass?帶了什麼化學物?」.Comprehensive MP studies typically employ both in parallel.

Py-GC-MS: Pyrolysis-Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry

Principle三步驟

  1. Pyrolysis：在無氧條件下將Sample 加熱至 500–700°C，聚合物斷裂成小分子（單體、寡聚物、副產物)
  2. GC separation: volatile pyrolysates separated on capillary column
  3. MS detection: EI ionization (70 eV); spectra matched against NIST library

Key hardware

  • Pyrolyzer: Curie-point or micro-furnace are the two mainstream designs
  • 典型Sample 量：0.1–50 μg is sufficient
  • detection limits：~0.1 μg per polymer
  • Analysis time: ~30–60 min per sample

Why "inert atmosphere"?

Pyrolysis must occur in an 惰性氣氛（He)下進行.若混入 O₂，聚合物會發生oxidative pyrolysis，產生大量 CO₂、H₂O and 非特異性氧化產物，破壞「指標化合物」的特異性，無法做定性與定量.

Käppler A. et al. (2018) Analysis of environmental microplastics by vibrational microspectroscopy: FTIR, Raman or both? Anal Bioanal Chem 408:8377–8391.

Py-GC-MS pyrolysate matching

某未知Sample 色譜圖最強峰質譜為 m/z = 104，伴隨 m/z = 208、312 較小峰群.主成分塑膠是?

Reasoning
  • PS is correct: m/z 104 is styrene monomer (C₈H₈)；208 是雙聚體 (1,3-diphenylpropane)、312 是三聚體.三者並現是 PS 的招牌.
  • PP 的指標 m/z = 126 (2,4-dimethyl-1-heptene).
  • PE primary為烷-烯-雙烯三聯體，由 C₆₊ 起，無單一強峰.
  • PET 為 148 (vinyl benzoate)、122 (benzoic acid).

Fischer M. & Scholz-Böttcher BM. (2017) Simultaneous Trace Identification and Quantification of Common Types of Microplastics in Environmental Samples by Pyrolysis-Gas Chromatography-Mass Spectrometry. Environ Sci Technol 51:5052–5060.

TED-GC-MS：可處理較大Sample 的進階版

Core difference

TED = Thermal Extraction Desorption.Sample 在thermogravimetric analyzer (TGA) under controlled heating, and the products are collected on a solid sorbent (PDMS strip) 蒐集，再以熱脫附導入 GC-MS.

  • Sample 量可達 100 mg（傳統 Py-GC-MS 僅 0.1–50 μg)
  • Suitable for direct injection of complex matrices（沉積物、土壤、消化後組織)
  • detection limits：~1 μg/g matrix

Scenario決策（一)：海鹽中的微塑膠

You have 500 g of commercial sea salt and wish to determine the type and particle count.請選最合適的primary analytical instrument:

Explanation
  • μ-FTIR + FPA is best：需要「類型 + 顆粒數」兩個資訊，且粒徑都在 FTIR detection limits (10–20 μm) 以上.FPA 化學成像可整片濾膜一次掃描、自動分類，highest efficiency.
  • ATR-FTIR：Suitable for 單顆 > 500 μm 顆粒，逐顆量太慢.
  • Raman：可行但分析時間長（每顆 30–60 秒，海鹽濾膜數百顆需數小時)；且部分海鹽顆粒帶色，螢光干擾.
  • Py-GC-MS：得到「總mass」但失去「顆粒數」資訊，不符問題需求.

Karami A. et al. (2017) The presence of microplastics in commercial salts from different countries. Sci Rep 7:46173.

Scenario決策（二)：瓶裝水中的奈米塑膠

Regulations require determination of the 總mass濃度（μg/L).請選最合適的方法組合：

Explanation
  • D is best：100 nm – 1 μm 顆粒已遠低於 FTIR and 常規 Raman 的detection limits；要的是「mass濃度」而非「顆數」 → Py-GC-MS is the first choice.pre-process用asymmetric flow field-flow fractionation (AF4) or ultracentrifugation將水中奈米塑膠濃縮.

  • 顯微 Raman：Explanation度 ~1 μm，邊界情況；數量太多無法計數，且即便用 SERS 也只能定性.

Schwaferts C. et al. (2019) Methods for the analysis of submicrometer- and nanoplastic particles. TrAC 112:52–65.

LC-MS：分析Plasticizers與寡聚物

In microplastic analysis, LC-MS does not directly measure "plastic particles". Instead, it measures the chemicals leached from plastics:

Plasticizers (Plasticizers)

phthalate esters (DEHP, DBP, BBP), citrate esters

Endocrine disruptors

BPA, BPS, BPF

Oligomers

PET cyclic trimer, PE waxy oligomers

Additives

antioxidants (Irganox 1010), UV stabilizers (Tinuvin series)

Typical LC-MS workflow and MRM quantification

Typical workflow

  1. Sample → 有機溶劑萃取（MeOH、ACN、IPA)
  2. Solid-phase extraction (SPE) cleanup (HLB, MCX)
  3. UHPLC C18 column separation
  4. ESI（+)或（−)離子化
  5. Triple-quadrupole (QqQ) MRM quantification

Plasticizers與 BPA：為什麼食品分析師最在乎這兩類?

Phthalate esters

  • Added to PVC, PET, PA to enhance flexibility
  • Non-covalent binding → readily migrates into food
  • 歐盟法規 (EU) 10/2011 嚴格規範：DEHP < 1.5 mg/kg food
  • Taiwan FDA: residue limits set for DEHP, DBP, BBP, DINP, DIDP, DnOP
  • 分析法：QuEChERS + GC-MS/MS or LC-MS/MS

Bisphenol A (BPA)

  • Monomer of PC baby bottles and epoxy can linings
  • Endocrine disruptor; banned in food-contact materials in the EU starting 2024（Reg (EU) 2024/3190)
  • Substitutes BPS, BPF have similar activity — should be monitored together
  • Analytical method: water sample → SPE → UHPLC-MS/MS (ESI−)

微塑膠顆粒分析 → Spectroscopy + Py-GC-MS；Plastic-derived chemical analysis → LC-MS.目標、Sample pre-process、儀器設定完全不同.

Cao XL. (2010) Phthalate esters in foods: sources, occurrence, and analytical methods. Compr Rev Food Sci Food Saf 9:21–43.

Method selection decision tree

mass化 vs 顆粒化：報數據時要注意什麼?

Particle-based

  • Unit: 顆數 / L、顆數 / g Sample
  • Source: FTIR, Raman chemical imaging
  • Pros: intuitive, size distribution available, morphology visualized
  • Cons: 隨儀器Explanation度而變（同一樣本，5 μm 截止 vs 20 μm 截止結果差 10–100 倍)

mass化 (Mass-based)

  • Unit: μg/L、μg/g Sample
  • Source: Py-GC-MS, TED-GC-MS
  • Pros: aligned with toxicology study units, comparable across labs
  • Cons: 失去顆粒尺寸、形貌資訊

Cowger et al. (2020) reporting guidelines

建議論文同時報告：① 偵測下限粒徑、② 顆數濃度（每升或每克)、③ mass濃度（若可得)、④ 鑑定方法（FTIR/Raman/Py-GC-MS)、⑤ 比對庫名稱、⑥ 空白控制組數據、⑦ 回收率（spike-recovery).

Cowger W. et al. (2020) Reporting Guidelines to Increase the Reproducibility and Comparability of Research on Microplastics. Appl Spectrosc 74:1066–1077.

文獻案例（一)：Yang et al. 中國市售食鹽

Study design

  • 採購 15 種中國市售食鹽（海鹽、湖鹽、岩鹽)
  • 250 g per sample dissolved in deionized water → filtered through 1.6 μm glass fiber filter
  • Microscopy counting + μ-FTIR identification (45–4500 μm range)

Key results

  • 海鹽：550–681 顆/kg（最高)
  • Lake salt: 43–364 particles/kg
  • Rock salt: 7–204 particles/kg
  • primary塑膠：PET, PE, cellophane

Methodology and QA/QC

  • μ-FTIR：粒徑 > 45 μm，可逐顆鑑定
  • 3 procedural blank filters per day for background subtraction
  • Fibers and fragments counted separately
  • 純棉實驗衣、避免塑膠容器（空氣纖維是最大污染源)

Yang D. et al. (2015) Microplastic Pollution in Table Salts from China. Environ Sci Technol 49:13622–13627. https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.est.5b03163

文獻案例（二)：Hernandez et al. 塑膠茶包釋出奈米塑膠

Study design

  • 四款市售「網狀」茶包（材質為 PA / PET)
  • Tea leaves removed and bags rinsed; immersed in 95 °C water for 5 min
  • Scanning electron microscopy (SEM) for morphology
  • μ-FTIR + μ-Raman for material identification
  • Nanoparticle tracking analysis (NTA) for counting

Striking findings

  • 每杯（一個茶包)釋出：
    ～1.16 × 10⁹ MP + 3.1 × 10⁹ NP
  • Released particles confirmed as PA + PET（與茶包本體一致)
  • Daphnia exposure: behavioral and developmental abnormalities

Methodological highlights

  • FTIR for large particles, Raman for 1–10 μm particles — complementary use
  • NTA 解決奈米計數問題（但無化學身份)

Hernandez LM. et al. (2019) Plastic Teabags Release Billions of Microparticles and Nanoparticles into Tea. Environ Sci Technol 53:12300–12310. https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.est.9b02540

文獻案例（三)：Ribeiro et al. 海鮮中的 Py-GC-MS 定量

Study design

  • Five Australian high-commercial-value seafoods: wild oysters, farmed shrimp, farmed crabs, farmed salmon, wild sardines
  • 10% KOH digestion, filtration, drying
  • Double-shot Py-GC-MS: 350 °C first to remove small molecules, then 700 °C for polymer pyrolysis
  • Simultaneous quantification of 5 plastics: PE, PP, PVC, PS, PMMA

結果（每克可食組織)

  • Sardines: 0.3 mg/g（最高，primary為 PVC)
  • Oysters: 0.04 mg/g
  • Shrimp: 0.07 mg/g
  • Crabs: 0.03 mg/g
  • 鮭魚：< detection limits

Methodological highlights

  • Quantification unit is μg/g, directly compatible with daily intake assessment
  • Use of ¹³C-PS internal standard校正基質效應
  • Recovery 80–110%

Ribeiro F. et al. (2020) Quantitative Analysis of Selected Plastics in High-Commercial-Value Australian Seafood by Pyrolysis Gas Chromatography Mass Spectrometry. Environ Sci Technol 54:9408–9417. https://doi.org/10.1021/acs.est.0c02337

Which primary儀器?Why?

Reference answer: μ-FTIR 化學成像（鑑定 1–500 μm 顆粒類型與計數)+ Py-GC-MS（總mass-based quantification)；NP 範圍補 SEM + EDS.

How would you design blanks and controls?
